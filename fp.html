Final Project

Main Focus: Building a lightsaber

:

This project had many steps. I began by doing a 3-D print for the handle, with Fusion 360 being my software, though this ended up being too small. I reprinted, editing the scale in maker-bot and created slots for some ports. I then purchased some battery powered LEDs and clear plastic tubing. I removed a case for the battery slots of the battery powered LEDs and sautered the LEDs to a smaller battery, and a switch. Around this time, I spray painted the light saber handle. Also, I found at this time, that the makerspace had some LED strips. I got one and sautered it to be blue, though I had some trouble with the wiring, so I resautered it to be green and sautered it to a sqitch as well. Next, I used the plastic tube and cut slots in it so I could pop the buttons through and leave the batteries in the handle. Finally I added a sticker I printed on the Roland 540-i. This with all the roadblocks took about a week.
